About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the CSE Department at Pennsylvania State University-University Park since the 2022 Fall, under the supervision of Prof. Abhinav Verma. I received my Master's and Bachelor's Degrees from the University of Science and Technology Beijing in 2022 and 2019, respectively. Back then, my supervisor was Prof. Xu-Cheng Yin.

My recent research focuses on Video Multimodal LLMs (Omni), Diffusion Models, and Reinforcement Learning.

Please refer to my resume for more details.
